About Us

The entire culinary world of India awaits hungry visitors at the Indian Cuisine at Kolkata, India.

The team has set itself a clear mission: We don't just want to be a very good Indian restaurant, we want to offer you an oasis of well-being where you'll be happy to linger. Our foods are tasteful, comfortable and designed with warm colors and we always pay attention to cordiality and friendly service. We also offer you extras such as a cocktail happy hour and the opportunity to spend an evening in our oriental lounge with a bowling alley.

Thank you for being our patron for 13 years.

Outdoor Catering

Indian cuisine has built up an enviable list of individual, corporate, celebrity and social clientele over a period of time. Each assignment is customized to suit the requirements of the host and the invitees.

We do our best to create the right ambience, keeping in mind the needs of the host and his invitee list to present a happy culinary experience, rather than just serving out food. In addition to Bengali, we also serve a range of other cuisines at our caterings viz, Indian, Tandoor as well as a wide array of popular street food. The Kolkata street food section is always an added attraction.

We welcome you to address your requirements for well planned menus and customized price offer to make your event a success. We would be happy to receive your enquiries. Our mail & contact information are: 'indiancuisine@gmail.com' or contact us at +91 123456789.
